KUNG FU PANDA TRILOGY

MOVIE REVIEW

Po, a Panda who works in his father's noodle shop, dreams of becoming a Kung Fu warrior. When Po learns that the village is hosting a tournament to nominate a Dragon Warrior, he rushes to the Jade Palace to watch and accidentally stumbles himself into the plot

The movie is elegantly drawn, action sequences with energy, and it's short enough to be forgiving, And the humor and quiet scenes sure pack a punch
